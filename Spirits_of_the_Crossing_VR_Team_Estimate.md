# Spirits of the Crossing — VR Team Estimate
*Prepared March 2026*

---

## What This Project Is

**Spirits of the Crossing** is a VR game built around a resonance-driven AI architecture in which players enter a ritual cave, express an internal resonance state, and travel to one of six living worlds — each inhabited by AI agents with distinct spirit archetypes drawn from Mayan cosmology. The world responds to the player's presence. Ancient myths activate as collective coherence builds. The universe has memory.

The project is technically unusually mature. The core simulation engine, world mythology system, cave ritual scene, portal selection layer, companion AI, and Elder Air Dragon realm loop already exist and run. Most games at this conceptual stage have none of that.

## The Honest Assessment

**Six people for 18 months** is the realistic path to a shippable VR experience. That is achievable because:

1. **The architecture is done.** Most games this complex spend 12–18 months just designing what this project has already built and proven in simulation.

2. **The cave scene already runs.** The ritual onboarding — the most important 10 minutes of any spiritual VR game — exists as a WebGL build. That is essentially the VR tutorial loop.

3. **The realm template exists.** The Elder Air Dragon loop proves the pattern. Remaining worlds inherit the same interface — they need art and content, not new systems.

4. **The mythology is internally consistent and documented.** Six worlds, nine archetypes, 20+ myth keys, Mayan cosmological correspondence — all authored and running.

---

## Highest-Risk Gaps

Three areas carry the most risk regardless of team size:

**1. VR performance**
Running six living worlds with 96 AI agents at 90fps in a head-mounted display is a real engineering challenge. The simulation is CPU-heavy by design. A dedicated VR engineer with profiling experience is non-negotiable.

**2. Spatial audio**
The resonance field system — oscillating Upsilon nodes, elemental world frequencies, brain coherence states — *demands* great sound design. This is the layer most likely to make the game feel like nothing else in VR. It is also the layer most often underbudgeted.

**3. Art pipeline**
The worlds are conceptually rich and precisely specified (ForestHeart/Ceiba roots, SourceVeil/Xibalba threshold, DarkContrast/Ah Puch fire passage). Realizing that in 3D assets that match the internal architecture is the largest single production cost.
