# Forest Finishing Touches

These are the strongest finishing touches for the current forest world.

## Atmosphere
- reflection pool clarity changes with calm and moon conditions
- mist layers react to spirit visibility
- fairy density rises in healthy zones
- gnome signs appear near roots, stones, and hidden caches
- water glow strengthens when healing succeeds

## Elder Spirit Stag
- first appears in reflection
- then at forest edge
- then near enough for witnessing/offering
- never forced, always conditional

## Player / AI / Animal / Spirit interaction
- player can ask, heal, wait, witness, share
- AI can interpret, assist, or stay back
- animal can approach, refuse, soothe, or guide
- spirit can reveal hidden pathways and timing

## Living forest actions
- heal dryads and trees
- ask vines/trees for food
- call or reveal water
- request hawk support
- restore damaged forest patches

## Memory
The forest remembers:
- what was healed
- what was asked
- what was given freely
- what was shared
- whether the player acted with patience or greed

## Best polish pass
- one complete spring restoration loop
- one elder stag encounter
- one food offering node
- one water call node
- one dryad/tree healing cluster
- one return portal outcome variation
